Game Showcase

This page highlights upcoming and current games, expansions, and experiences planned for The Red – RRG Mobile.

The L&E – Virtual Train Experience
A virtual train tour through the evolving world of Camp Red.

The L&E allows players to ride across the digital Red River Gorge as it develops in real time. New locations, biomes, and features are revealed as they are created in the cloud, offering both an immersive journey and a live progress preview for supporters.

Camp Red – Camping Simulator
The foundation for future Red River Gorge projects.

Camp Red serves as the main framework for connected experiences including Swift, Deep Red, Rapid Red, and virtual tours. Players can build campsites, gather resources, craft tools, and explore a living, evolving world. This AAA-scale sandbox emphasizes realism, relaxation, and discovery.

Swift – Story Campaign
Expands Camp Red with a fully story-driven campaign and team play.

Swift introduces a story-driven adventure about treasure hunters seeking John Swift's lost silver in the Appalachian wilds. Features include handcrafted environments, story locations, metal detection, excavation mechanics, and advanced survival tools. Each update expands the legend with mysteries and moral choices.

The Darkening – Standalone Horror Experience
Set in the Red River Gorge, this horror title from Periltek combines first-person, point & click, and 2D/2.5D gameplay.

Overview: Explore the RRG in a suspenseful survival horror story. The game features a fully immersive world where darkness is a tangible threat. Episodes are released via a live story system, allowing players to download and experience the narrative piece by piece, like a serialized anthology.

Gameplay: Players must survive using light-based mechanics, manage inventory and campsite, and navigate the episodic story. The mobile version lets you play as the author leaving journals behind, which the PC version discovers, creating interconnected storytelling.

Characters & Lore: Interact with NPCs of uncertain allegiance. Discover journals, radios, and environmental storytelling that reveal backstories and local myths. Some characters may only exist through found notes or cryptic clues.

Art & Atmosphere: Dark, atmospheric visuals with realistic and stylized lighting. Sound design enhances tension with nature ambiance, distant chaos, and radio static. UI is minimal and diegetic, immersing players in the world.

Release Plan: Episodic mobile prequel (Unity Addressables) first, followed by full HD PC release and potential console adaptation.
