Welcome to The Red

The Red – RRG Mobile is the first free, information-driven application dedicated to the Red River Gorge region. It delivers verified regional data, digital directories, interactive maps, and live updates.

The app gamifies the Gorge experience: explore verified locations, track activities, and enjoy mini-games and pastimes designed for leisure and discovery.

Core capabilities include live bulletins, interactive documents, searchable contacts, employer listings, tourism guides, offline access, and reporting tools — all curated for easy navigation and safety.

App Highlights

Streamlined Interface — Quick access to reliable Gorge-wide information.

Navigation is optimized for speed and simplicity across all devices, allowing users to access bulletins, directories, maps, and games efficiently.

Dynamic Updates — Real-time bulletins for safety, weather, and events.

Powered by LiveLink, updates are automated and verified for accuracy, giving users access to the latest trail, road, and community information.

Unified Directory — Connect to local businesses and tourism resources.

Includes verified listings for dining, lodging, activities, and community services, making it simple to plan your visit or daily routine.

Community Support — Promoting authentic regional businesses and events.

The app spotlights verified local entities, community gatherings, and partnerships that help sustain the Gorge’s economic and environmental ecosystem.

About the Project

The Red is part of the RRG Mobile Initiative — a digital information project connecting community data, tourism resources, and workforce information for the Red River Gorge region.
