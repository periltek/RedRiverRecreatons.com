Planned Features
  • Interactive Area Guide: Explore each section of the Gorge with detailed information, maps, and hidden gems.
  • Mini-Games: Enjoy engaging games like Red Racer (multiplayer RC car racing) and Boulder Roller (navigate challenging boulder courses).
  • Real-Time Weather: Stay updated with accurate weather forecasts to plan your adventures.
  • Local Insights: Uncover tips, hidden spots, and secrets shared by locals.
  • Dynamic Maps: Navigate trails, parks, and attractions with live updates (When Available)
  • Educational Content: Learn about the myths, legends, and history of the Red River Gorge.
  • Rewards and Easter Eggs: Unlock secret information and special content by completing challenges.
  • Offline Access: Save guides and maps for use even without an internet connection.
  • User-Friendly Design: Enjoy effortless navigation with a sleek, intuitive interface.
  • Support and Feedback: Contact the developers or share your thoughts directly through the app.
  • Careers: Potential new Work Opportunities
    • Local Guides: Advertise within the app for free to boost your career. Some restrictions may apply. Contact us for more information and discover how we can help you grow with us.
    • Event Coordinators, Local Content Creators & Community Managers: You’re welcome to advertise or share educational content on The Red app for free.
    • Cabin and Lodge Hosts: Advertise your services in a planned update set for 2025 (Q3–Q4). For the latest updates on this feature and others, reach out to us and see how we can support your growth.
    • Local Classifieds: Launching in 2024 (Q3), this feature will allow locals, businesses, and visitors to browse and post listings. Some restrictions may apply. Contact us for further details.
    • Cabin & Campton Cab Company Drivers: Launching in 2026 (Q2–Q3), this exciting startup will shuttle tourists between cabins, campgrounds, trailheads, and attractions. Stay tuned for updates & be among the first to know more when CabinCab.com and CamptonCab.com go live! Contact us for the latest news.

What We Are About

At Red River Recreations, our mission is to enhance your experience in the Red River Gorge by providing an all-in-one digital guide through The Red's Mobile App. We aim to connect adventurers, locals, and nature enthusiasts with the stunning beauty of the Gorge, offering helpful resources, engaging mini-games, real-time weather updates, and much more. Our goal is to foster a deeper connection to this beautiful natural space, providing interactive features, educational content, and hidden gems that will inspire you to explore, learn, and enjoy. We're committed to sustainability, promoting local businesses, and empowering the community to experience the Gorge like never before.
