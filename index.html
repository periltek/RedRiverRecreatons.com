Planned Features

01

Interactive Area Guide: Explore each section of the Gorge with detailed information, maps, and hidden gems, one page at a time.

02

Mini-Games: Enjoy engaging games like Red Racer (multiplayer RC car racing) and Boulder Roller (navigate challenging boulder courses).

03

Weather Updates: Stay updated with accurate & regular updates regarding RRG's weather to plan your adventures with ease of mind.
Live Weather Updates [WIP]

04

Local Insights: Uncover tips, hidden spots, and secrets shared by locals with Dynamic Maps. Navigate trails, parks, and attractions with updates. 

05

Educational Content: Learn about the myths, legends, and history of the Red River Gorge. We plan to cram as much literature as we can to give you the whole narrative.

06

Trail Maps: Access consolidated trail map sources through a simple, user-friendly search. Search for a trail and be directed to the Department of Forestry's official map download page.

07

Accessibility Support: Effortlessly activate volume boost, narration, advanced font settings, brightness settings, high contrast options, and full color controls for a more personalized and accessible experience.

Free Marketing & Local Classifieds for The Red's locals, businesses, and visitors.

What We Are About

At Red River Recreations, our mission is to enhance your experience in the Red River Gorge by providing an all-in-one digital guide through The Red's Mobile App. We aim to connect adventurers, locals, and nature enthusiasts with the stunning beauty of the Gorge, offering helpful resources, engaging mini-games, real-time weather updates, and much more. Our goal is to foster a deeper connection to this beautiful natural space, providing interactive features, educational content, and hidden gems that will inspire you to explore, learn, and enjoy. We're committed to sustainability, promoting local businesses, and empowering the community to experience the Gorge like never before.
