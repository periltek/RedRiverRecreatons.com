Planned Features
  • Interactive Area Guide: Explore each section of the Gorge with in-depth details, maps, and hidden gems.
  • Mini-Games: Play engaging games like Red Racer (multiplayer RC car racing) and Boulder Roller (navigate boulder challenges).
  • Real-Time Weather: Get accurate weather updates to plan your adventures effectively.
  • Local Insights: Discover tips, hidden spots, and secrets shared by locals.
  • Dynamic Maps: Navigate trails, parks, and attractions with live updates.
  • Educational Content: Access myths, legends, and the history of the Red River Gorge.
  • Rewards and Easter Eggs: Unlock secret information and special content through challenges.
  • Offline Access: Save guides and maps for use even without internet connectivity.
  • User-Friendly Design: Navigate the app effortlessly with an intuitive, sleek interface.
  • Support and Feedback: Reach out to developers or share your thoughts directly through the app.
Careers: Potential new Work Opportunities
  • Local Adventure Guides
  • Cabin Cab Company Drivers: Shuttling tourists between cabins, trailheads, and attractions. (CabinCab.Com)
  • Campton Cab Company: Broader transportation, including airport pickups and long-haul trips. (CamptonCab.Com)
  • Event Coordinators which will organize community events, workshops, and hikes, promoted via the app, with partnerships with local businesses.
  • Curators which will validate and update local data, maps, and attractions.
  • Trail Monitors which will verify trail conditions and accessibility in real-time, ensuring accurate data updates in the app.
  • App Support Specialists -In House -to assist users with app navigation, troubleshooting, and account management. (Tech Support)
  • Field Technicians - Maintain tech infrastructure for app-dependent services such as dynamic maps or location tracking and a planned LoFi wireless network in the RRG
  • Brand Ambassadors to promote the app and related services at regional events and online, fostering a larger user base.
  • Community Managers - which will engage with users, collect feedback, and build a strong community around the app.


Advertising: How much will it cost to place an ad in the Red Mobile?

For local residents, businesses, visitors, & general tourists, the cost to advertise will be donation based only. Meaning free if you want or the price of a cup of coffee if you feel inclined. We aren't picky.

What We Are About

At Red River Recreations, our mission is to enhance your experience in the Red River Gorge by providing an all-in-one digital guide through The Red's Mobile App. We aim to connect adventurers, locals, and nature enthusiasts with the stunning beauty of the Gorge, offering helpful resources, engaging mini-games, real-time weather updates, and much more. Our goal is to foster a deeper connection to this beautiful natural space, providing interactive features, educational content, and hidden gems that will inspire you to explore, learn, and enjoy. We're committed to sustainability, promoting local businesses, and empowering the community to experience the Gorge like never before.
