Privacy & Security Policy

The Red – RRG Mobile respects your privacy. We do not collect personal data from users. All app functions operate without requesting or storing identifiable user information.

Data Handling
How The Red manages information.

The app only accesses cloud or remote LiveLink data needed to operate and display content. It does not upload any user data beyond the device’s necessary functions.

Public & Commercial Information
Content included in the app.

The Red houses publicly listed and commercial information, such as contact details, business descriptions, locations, and recreational opportunities. All data is fully sourced and verifiable, but user identities are never exposed.

Team & Volunteer Privacy
Protecting internal identities.

Volunteer and team identities are never shared unless explicitly requested by the individual or an authorized representative in person. We maintain strict confidentiality of our contributors.

Local Storage & Caching
Temporary offline access.

Some content like images, audio, video, or contact information may be cached or stored locally to improve performance and provide offline access. This data does not include user identity.

Security Practices
Keeping the app secure.

All data handled by the app is protected using standard encryption when transmitted, and cached content is securely stored. No personal user information is collected, shared, or sold.
